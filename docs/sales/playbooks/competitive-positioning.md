# Competitive Positioning

How we win against the competition.

## Competitor Landscape

### 1. Traditional Lead Aggregators (e.g., LendingTree, EverQuote)
- **Their Weakness**: Shared leads, low intent, high cost, manual follow-up required.
- **Our Edge**: Exclusive, real-time leads, automated qualification, and CRM integration.
- **Winning Argument**: "Why pay for leads that 5 other agencies are also calling? Own the customer journey from the first click."

### 2. General CRM/Marketing Automation (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce)
- **Their Weakness**: High complexity, requires significant setup for insurance-specific needs, no built-in lead generation.
- **Our Edge**: Vertical-specific (built for insurance), turnkey solution, includes lead gen + automation.
- **Winning Argument**: "HubSpot is a great tool, but it's a blank slate. Our platform is purpose-built for insurance workflows on day one."

### 3. Specialized Insurance CRM (e.g., AgencyBloc, EZLynx)
- **Their Weakness**: Great for policy management, but often weak on front-end lead generation and modern AI automation.
- **Our Edge**: Superior AI-driven lead qualification and real-time distribution.
- **Winning Argument**: "Keep your policy management system, but use us for your growth engine. We integrate with the tools you already use."

| Feature | Competitors | Our Platform |

## Win/Loss Analysis (Common Themes)

### Why We Win:
- **Speed to Value**: Customers see leads flowing within 48 hours.
- **Ease of Use**: Agents actually like using the interface.
- **Specific ROI**: The cost per acquisition (CPA) is lower than their current baseline.

### Why We Lose:
- **Budget**: Smaller agencies simply don't have the monthly spend.
- **Legacy Systems**: Deep integration with very old legacy systems that we don't support yet.
- **Internal Politics**: Agency has a "homegrown" system they are attached to.
