# Deal Structure & Pricing

## Pricing Model

Our pricing is designed to scale with the agency's growth.

### 1. Platform Fee (Monthly SaaS)
- **Starter**: $499/mo (Up to 5 agents)
- **Professional**: $999/mo (Up to 20 agents)
- **Enterprise**: $2,499/mo (Unlimited agents, custom integrations)

### 2. Lead Spend (Variable)
- Clients can bring their own spend (managed by us) or use our direct lead purchase program.
- 15% Management fee on managed ad spend.

### 3. Implementation Fee (One-time)
- **Standard**: $1,500 (Setup, CRM integration, training)
- **Enterprise**: $5,000+ (Custom workflows, data migration)

## Deal Structure Templates

### The "Growth Accelerator" Bundle
- **Target**: Agencies looking to scale.
- **Includes**: Professional SaaS, $5k/mo managed lead spend, 2-day on-site training.
- **Contract Term**: 12 Months.

### The "SaaS Only" Setup
- **Target**: Tech-savvy agencies who manage their own ads.
- **Includes**: Starter or Professional SaaS, API access.
- **Contract Term**: Month-to-month or Annual.

## Negotiation Levers
1. **Contract Length**: Offer a 15-20% discount for annual upfront payment.
2. **Implementation Waiver**: Waive the setup fee for multi-year commitments.
3. **Pilot Program**: 30-day "proof of concept" with a smaller platform fee to prove lead quality.

## Discounting Policy
- Up to 10%: AE Discretion.
- 11% - 25%: Sales Director Approval.
- 25%+: VP Sales / CEO Approval.
