# Sales Cycle & Stage Gates

## Typical Timeline (Average: 45 Days)

| Week | Activity | Goal |
|------|----------|------|
| Week 1 | Discovery & Qualification | Qualify the lead and schedule demo. |
| Week 2 | Product Demo | Build value and identify stakeholders. |
| Week 3 | Technical Validation | Confirm CRM/Data requirements. |
| Week 4 | Proposal & ROI Review | Submit formal bid and handle objections. |
| Week 5 | Negotiation | Finalize price and terms. |
| Week 6 | Closing & Sign-off | Contract signature and payment. |

## Detailed Stage Gates

### Stage 1: Prospecting (10%)
- **Entry**: New lead from marketing or outbound effort.
- **Exit**: Contact made, discovery call scheduled.

### Stage 2: Discovery (25%)
- **Entry**: Discovery call started.
- **Exit**: BANT criteria met, pain points documented, demo scheduled.

### Stage 3: Demo/Evaluation (50%)
- **Entry**: Demo completed.
- **Exit**: "Technical Win" confirmed, key stakeholders bought in.

### Stage 4: Proposal/Quote (75%)
- **Entry**: Formal proposal sent.
- **Exit**: Proposal reviewed with decision maker, initial verbal agreement.

### Stage 5: Negotiation/Legal (90%)
- **Entry**: Contract sent for review.
- **Exit**: Legal/Procurement approval.

### Stage 6: Closed Won (100%)
- **Entry**: Signature received.
- **Exit**: Kickoff call scheduled, handoff to CS.

## Pipeline Management
- Review deals weekly with Sales Manager.
- Ensure all fields in CRM are updated (Close Date, Amount, Next Step).
- Flag any "at-risk" deals early for management support.
