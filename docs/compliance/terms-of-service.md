# Terms of Service (Template)

This document is a starting template for customer-facing terms of service. It should be reviewed by legal counsel.

## Key Sections to Include

- Service description
- Acceptable use
- Customer responsibilities
- Fees, billing, refunds (if applicable)
- Data ownership and processing
- Confidentiality
- Warranties and disclaimers
- Limitation of liability
- Termination
