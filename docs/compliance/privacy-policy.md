# Privacy Policy (Template)

This document is a starting template for a customer-facing privacy policy. It should be reviewed by legal counsel and tailored to your jurisdiction, data flows, and vendors.

## What We Collect
- Account and profile data
- Lead and campaign data
- Usage analytics and device data (where enabled)

## How We Use Data
- Provide and operate the service
- Improve product quality and reliability
- Support and incident response

## Data Sharing
- Subprocessors (hosting, email/SMS/voice, analytics)
- Legal compliance requests

## Data Retention
- Defined retention window per data type
- Deletion workflows for DSRs

## Contact
Provide a privacy contact email and DPO contact (if applicable).
