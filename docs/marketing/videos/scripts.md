# Video Production Briefs & Scripts

## 1. Product Overview (2 Minutes)
- **Goal**: High-level introduction to the platform.
- **Visuals**: Animated UI walkthrough + voiceover.
- **Script Outline**:
    - [0:00-0:15] The problem: Agents drowning in bad leads.
    - [0:15-0:45] The solution: Introducing [Platform Name].
    - [0:45-1:30] Key Features: AI scoring, Real-time distribution, CRM sync.
    - [1:30-2:00] Call to Action: Visit our website for a demo.

## 2. Customer Testimonial (3 Minutes)
- **Goal**: Build trust through peer validation.
- **Visuals**: Interview with an agency owner + shots of their team working.
- **Key Questions**:
    - "What was life like before the platform?"
    - "What was the implementation process like?"
    - "What's the one feature your agents can't live without?"
    - "What's the ROI you've seen so far?"

## 3. "How It Works" (Shorts/Reels)
- **Goal**: Quick educational snippets for social media.
- **Topics**:
    - How AI scores a lead in 2 seconds.
    - Setting up your first automated SMS nurture.
    - Reading your ROI dashboard.
