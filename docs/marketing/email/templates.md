# Email Campaign Templates

Standardized email sequences for various stages of the customer journey.

## 1. New Lead Nurture (The "Awareness" Sequence)

### Email 1: The Welcome
- **Subject**: Stop chasing leads, start closing policies.
- **Body**: Hi [Name], I noticed you're looking to scale your agency. Most owners we talk to are frustrated with shared leads and manual follow-up. We've built a better way.

### Email 2: The Proof
- **Subject**: How [Agency X] grew by 40% in 90 days.
- **Body**: Hi [Name], following up on my last email. I wanted to share a quick story of how another agency owner solved their lead quality problem using our platform...

### Email 3: The Call to Action
- **Subject**: Is your agency ready for AI?
- **Body**: Hi [Name], ready to see how it works? Let's spend 15 minutes next week showing you the platform in action. [Link to Calendar]

## 2. Post-Demo Follow-up

### Email 1: The Recap
- **Subject**: Recap of our demo / Next steps
- **Body**: Hi [Name], enjoyed showing you the platform today. As discussed, here are the technical specs and the implementation timeline we talked about...

## 3. Re-engagement (Cold Leads)

### Email 1: The "Still Interested?"
- **Subject**: Still looking for more leads?
- **Body**: Hi [Name], it's been a while. Since we last spoke, we've added [New Feature] which has been a game-changer for our clients. Worth a 5-minute chat?

## 4. Newsletter (Monthly)
- **Subject**: The Modern Agent Monthly - [Month] Edition
- **Body**: [Featured Blog Post] | [Product Update] | [Customer Spotlight]
