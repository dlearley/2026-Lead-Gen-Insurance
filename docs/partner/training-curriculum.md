# Partner Training Curriculum

## Overview
The Insurance Lead Generation AI Platform training curriculum is designed to equip partners with the knowledge and skills needed to maximize platform adoption, efficiency, and ROI.

## Training Program Structure

### 1. Role-Based Training Paths

#### Agent Training (Frontline Users)
- **Duration**: 8 hours
- **Format**: Self-paced online + 2 live sessions
- **Focus**: Day-to-day platform usage, lead management, basic reporting

#### Manager Training (Team Leaders)
- **Duration**: 12 hours
- **Format**: Self-paced online + 3 live sessions + workshop
- **Focus**: Team performance, advanced reporting, workflow optimization

#### Admin Training (System Administrators)
- **Duration**: 16 hours
- **Format**: Self-paced online + 4 live sessions + hands-on lab
- **Focus**: System configuration, user management, integration setup

### 2. Core Training Modules

#### Module 1: Platform Fundamentals
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Objective**: Introduction to platform navigation and basic concepts
- **Topics**:
  - Platform architecture and components
  - User interface overview
  - Navigation and workspace customization
  - Basic settings and preferences
  - Mobile app introduction

#### Module 2: Lead Management Best Practices
- **Duration**: 3 hours
- **Objective**: Master lead qualification, follow-up, and conversion
- **Topics**:
  - Lead lifecycle and status management
  - Lead qualification frameworks
  - Prioritization and scoring
  - Follow-up strategies and cadences
  - Conversion optimization techniques
  - Lead nurturing best practices

#### Module 3: CRM Integration Training
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Objective**: Seamless integration with partner CRM systems
- **Topics**:
  - CRM connection setup
  - Data synchronization configuration
  - Lead mapping and field alignment
  - Bi-directional data flow
  - Conflict resolution strategies
  - CRM-specific workflows (Salesforce, HubSpot, etc.)

#### Module 4: Advanced Features
- **Duration**: 3 hours
- **Objective**: Utilize advanced platform capabilities
- **Topics**:
  - Advanced filtering and search
  - Automation rules and workflows
  - Custom reporting and dashboards
  - API integration and webhooks
  - AI-powered lead scoring
  - Predictive analytics

#### Module 5: Compliance and Data Privacy
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Objective**: Ensure regulatory compliance and data security
- **Topics**:
  - Insurance industry regulations
  - Data privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  - Platform security features
  - Audit logging and compliance reporting
  - Data retention policies
  - Incident response procedures

#### Module 6: Performance Optimization
- **Duration**: 2 hours
- **Objective**: Maximize platform ROI and efficiency
- **Topics**:
  - Performance metrics and KPIs
  - Benchmarking and goal setting
  - Continuous improvement strategies
  - User adoption techniques
  - Platform updates and new features
  - Best practices sharing

## Certification Program

### Certification Tiers

#### Basic Certification
- **Prerequisites**: Complete Platform Fundamentals module
- **Exam**: 30 questions, 80% passing score
- **Validity**: 1 year
- **Renewal**: Complete annual refresher course

#### Advanced Certification
- **Prerequisites**: Basic Certification + 3 additional modules
- **Exam**: 50 questions, 85% passing score
- **Practical**: Hands-on assessment
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Renewal**: Complete 2 continuing education courses

#### Expert Certification
- **Prerequisites**: Advanced Certification + all modules
- **Exam**: 75 questions, 90% passing score
- **Case Study**: Real-world implementation analysis
- **Presentation**: Best practice sharing session
- **Validity**: 2 years
- **Renewal**: Complete 3 continuing education courses + mentor new partners

### Certification Benefits
- **Badges and Recognition**: Digital badges for email signatures and profiles
- **Priority Support**: Faster response times for certified users
- **Exclusive Content**: Access to advanced materials and beta features
- **Networking Opportunities**: Invitation to certified user events
- **Continuing Education**: Free access to new training modules
