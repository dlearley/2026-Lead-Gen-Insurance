# Customer Tier Definitions

## Overview

This document defines the customer tier structure, service levels, and resource allocations for different customer segments. Tiers ensure appropriate service delivery and support based on customer value and needs.

---

## Tier Summary

| Tier | Annual Revenue | Customer Count | CSM Ratio | Support SLA | Primary Focus |
|------|----------------|----------------|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| Tier 1 - Enterprise | $100,000+ | 1-5 | 1:1 Dedicated | < 2 hours | Strategic partnership |
| Tier 2 - Mid-Market | $25,000 - $99,999 | 10-20 | 1:5 Shared | < 4 hours | Growth & adoption |
| Tier 3 - Small Business | $5,000 - $24,999 | 50-100 | 1:20 Grouped | < 24 hours | Self-service enablement |
| Tier 4 - Trial/Freemium | $0 - $4,999 | Unlimited | Automated | 48+ hours | Conversion to paid |

## Tier 1: Enterprise

### Profile

**Target Customers**:
- Large insurance brokerages
- National carriers
- Multi-location agencies
- High-volume lead generators

**Characteristics**:
- Annual contract value: $100,000+
- 100+ licensed agents
- High lead volume (10,000+ per month)
- Complex integration requirements
- Multiple stakeholders and departments
- Strategic business relationship

### Service Model

#### Customer Success Management
- **Dedicated CSM**: 1:1 ratio
- **Dedicated Technical Account Manager**: For complex integrations
- **Executive Sponsor**: VP-level internal sponsor
- **Quarterly Business Reviews**: Strategic level
- **Monthly Business Reviews**: Operational level
- **Weekly tactical check-ins**: As needed

#### Support Services
- **Response SLA**: < 2 hours for critical issues
- **Resolution SLA**: < 24 hours for critical issues
- **24/7 phone support**: For critical issues
- **Dedicated support queue**: Priority routing
- **Technical Account Manager**: Complex issues
- **On-site support**: As needed (quarterly visits)

#### Onboarding
- **Duration**: 30-45 days
- **Executive kickoff**: In-person preferred
- **Dedicated onboarding specialist**: Full-time during onboarding
- **Custom training program**: Tailored to organization
- **Hands-on workshops**: On-site or virtual
- **Data migration**: Full-service migration
- **Integration setup**: Comprehensive support
- **UAT support**: Dedicated QA resources

### Features & Capabilities

#### Core Platform Features
✅ All standard features included
✅ Unlimited agents
✅ Unlimited leads
✅ Unlimited storage

#### Enterprise-Exclusive Features
✅ Custom integrations
✅ API rate limits: 10,000 requests/hour
✅ Advanced analytics and BI
✅ Custom reports and dashboards
✅ White-label branding options
✅ SSO with SAML 2.0
✅ Advanced security features
✅ Dedicated database instance
✅ Custom SLA guarantees
✅ Early access to new features
✅ Beta program participation

### Success Activities

#### Regular Touchpoints

|-----------|----------|----------|----------|

| Monthly | Business review | Managers | 60 min |

#### Proactive Activities
- Health score monitoring: Daily
- Usage analytics: Weekly

- Risk assessment: Weekly

- Annual roadmap review

#### Customer Advocacy
- Customer Advisory Board: Participation guaranteed

## Tier Transition Paths

### Upgrading Tiers

#### Tier 4 → Tier 3
**Trigger**:
- Exceeds freemium limits for 2 consecutive months
- Requests paid features
- Shows strong conversion signals

**Process**:
1. Automated notification
2. Sales outreach within 24 hours
3. Special upgrade offer
4. Smooth transition process

#### Tier 3 → Tier 2
**Trigger**:
- Exceeds Tier 3 limits consistently
- Requests mid-market features
- Annual revenue potential > $25,000

**Process**:
1. CSM identifies opportunity
2. Value demonstration
3. Upgrade proposal
4. Executive presentation
5. Smooth migration to new tier

#### Tier 2 → Tier 1
**Trigger**:
- Exceeds Tier 2 limits
- Requests enterprise features
- Annual revenue potential > $100,000
- Multiple locations/departments

**Process**:
1. VP Customer Success involved
2. Strategic assessment
3. Custom proposal
4. Executive sponsorship
5. Dedicated onboarding team

### Downgrading Tiers

#### Tier 1 → Tier 2
**Trigger**:
- Contract renewal with reduced commitment
- Business downsizing
- Mergers/acquisitions

**Process**:
1. 90-day notice period
2. Impact assessment
3. Service level adjustment
4. Team transition plan
5. Retention efforts

#### Tier 2 → Tier 3
**Trigger**:
- Contract renewal with reduced commitment
- Reduced usage needs
- Budget constraints

**Process**:
1. 60-day notice period
2. Feature adjustment
3. Transition support
4. Retention efforts

#### Tier 3 → Tier 4
**Trigger**:
- Non-renewal of paid subscription
- Business closure
- Change in business model

**Process**:
1. 30-day notice period
2. Data export assistance
3. Transition to freemium if desired
4. Reactivation efforts

## Tier Assignment Criteria

### Initial Tier Assignment

#### Tier 1 Assignment Criteria
- Annual budget: $100,000+
- Employee count: 100+ licensed agents
- Lead volume: 10,000+ per month
- Industry: Enterprise brokerage or carrier
- Geographic: Multiple locations/states
- Technology: Complex integration needs

#### Tier 2 Assignment Criteria
- Annual budget: $25,000 - $99,999
- Employee count: 10-99 licensed agents
- Lead volume: 1,000-9,999 per month
- Industry: Regional agency
- Geographic: Single region or multiple states
- Technology: Standard integration needs

#### Tier 3 Assignment Criteria
- Annual budget: $5,000 - $24,999
- Employee count: 1-9 licensed agents
- Lead volume: < 1,000 per month
- Industry: Local agency or independent
- Geographic: Single location
- Technology: Basic integration needs

#### Tier 4 Assignment Criteria
- Annual budget: <$5,000 or evaluating
- Employee count: 1-3 users
- Lead volume: < 100 per month
- Industry: Any
- Geographic: Any
- Technology: No integration needs

### Re-evaluation Criteria

Tiers are reviewed quarterly with potential for adjustment based on:

1. **Revenue Performance**
   - Consistent overachievement: Consider upgrade
   - Consistent underachievement: Consider downgrade

2. **Usage Patterns**
   - Consistently exceeding limits: Upgrade recommendation
   - Consistently underutilizing: Tier optimization discussion

3. **Business Changes**
   - Mergers/acquisitions: Re-assessment
   - Expansion: Upgrade opportunity
   - Downsizing: Downgrade discussion

4. **Strategic Alignment**
   - Strategic partnerships: Upgrade consideration
   - Brand ambassador: Enhanced tier benefits

## Service Level Agreement (SLA) Details

### Response Time Commitments

| Priority Level | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 | Tier 4 |
|----------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Critical (P1) - System down | < 2 hours | < 4 hours | < 8 hours | N/A |
| High (P2) - Major feature unavailable | < 4 hours | < 8 hours | < 24 hours | N/A |
| Medium (P3) - Minor issues | < 24 hours | < 24 hours | < 48 hours | 48+ hours |
| Low (P4) - Questions/inquiries | < 48 hours | < 72 hours | < 72 hours | 72+ hours |

### Resolution Time Commitments

| Priority Level | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 | Tier 4 |
|----------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Critical (P1) | < 24 hours | < 48 hours | < 72 hours | N/A |
| High (P2) | < 48 hours | < 72 hours | < 7 days | N/A |
| Medium (P3) | < 7 days | < 14 days | < 14 days | Best effort |
| Low (P4) | < 14 days | < 21 days | < 30 days | Best effort |

### Uptime Commitments

| Tier | Uptime Guarantee | Credit % |
|------|-----------------|----------|
| Tier 1 | 99.95% | 10% for < 99.95%, 25% for < 99.9%, 50% for < 99.5% |
| Tier 2 | 99.9% | 10% for < 99.9%, 25% for < 99.5%, 50% for < 99% |
| Tier 3 | 99.5% | 10% for < 99.5%, 25% for < 99% |
| Tier 4 | Best effort | No credit |
