# Knowledge Transfer & Handoff Plan

## 1. Objectives

- Ensure the receiving team has full operational knowledge of the Insurance Lead Gen Platform.
- Minimize downtime and risk during the transition of responsibilities.
- Establish clear support and escalation paths post-handoff.

## 2. Participants

- **Relinquishing Team**: Current development and operations team (Platform Architects, SREs).
- **Receiving Team**: New operational management team (DevOps Engineers, Support Engineers).

## 3. Timeline (15 Business Days)

| Week   | Phase                | Key Activities                                                              |
| ------ | -------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Week 1 | Documentation Review | Receiving team reviews all runbooks, SOPs, and architecture docs.           |
| Week 2 | Deep Dive Sessions   | Structured walkthroughs of each component (AI, Queues, DB, K8s).            |
| Week 3 | Shadowing            | Receiving team shadows current operators during daily checks and incidents. |
| Week 4 | Reverse Shadowing    | Receiving team performs tasks while current operators observe.              |
| Day 15 | Official Handover    | Final sign-off and transfer of primary on-call responsibilities.            |

## 4. Training Modules

1. **System Architecture & Data Flow** (3 hours)
2. **Infrastructure & Kubernetes Management** (4 hours)
3. **Monitoring, Alerting & Observability** (3 hours)
4. **Lead Lifecycle & AI Workflows** (4 hours)
5. **Incident Response & Disaster Recovery** (3 hours)

## 5. Success Criteria

- [ ] Receiving team has successfully completed the Readiness Certification.
- [ ] All operational documents are reviewed and verified as up-to-date.
- [ ] Receiving team has successfully handled at least 3 minor incidents or maintenance tasks.
- [ ] Support escalation matrix is active and tested.

## 6. Post-Handoff Support

For 30 days following the official handover, the relinquishing team will provide:

- **Level 3 Support**: 24/7 availability for SEV-1 incidents.
- **Weekly Sync**: A 1-hour meeting to discuss operational trends and questions.
- **Documentation Updates**: Assistance in refining runbooks based on new team feedback.
